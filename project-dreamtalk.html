Project Overview

The Challenge

Standard language models are trained on logical, structured text (Wikipedia, books). However, dreams are chaotic, non-linear, and deeply symbolic. The challenge was to force a logical model (GPT-2) to "unlearn" structure and embrace the surreal narrative style of human dreams without descending into gibberish.

The Solution: Fine-Tuning & Streamlit

I fine-tuned OpenAI's GPT-2 Small on the DreamBank dataset (27k+ dream reports). To ensure high-quality outputs, I implemented:

  • Quantitative Evaluation: Achieved a low Perplexity score (~23.17) and a high BERTScore F1 (0.85), ensuring the text is both creative and semantically sound.
  • Custom Narrative Logic: Developed a regex-based complete_dream() function to dynamically generate natural, dream-like endings.
  • Interactive UI: Built a Streamlit app allowing users to control "Creativity" (Temperature) and "Randomness" (Top-K/P) in real-time.
