Project Overview

Summary

We developed a sophisticated hybrid model combining ARIMA, Random Forest, and LSTM, enhanced with a meta-learner, to forecast stock prices with superior accuracy. This work addresses the volatility of financial markets by leveraging the strengths of both statistical and deep learning approaches.

My Contribution

My role was to establish the foundational research and methodological strategy that guided our model's development. By synthesizing complex literature, I helped define the architectural approach that ultimately led to our high-accuracy results. I also had the honor of presenting our findings at the conference.

What I Learned

This project instilled a drive for excellence through relentless perseverance, proving that innovation emerges from pushing through complex academic and technical challenges. This blend of technical tenacity and collaborative spirit has prepared me to thrive in rigorous engineering environments.
