Project Summary

We developed a sophisticated hybrid model combining ARIMA, Random Forest, and LSTM, enhanced with a meta-learner, to forecast stock prices with superior accuracy. This work was accepted into the ICCSCI 2025 and is under publication by Elsevier.

My Impact & Presentation

My role was to establish the foundational research and methodological strategy that guided our model's development. By synthesizing complex literature, I helped define the architectural approach that ultimately led to our high-accuracy results. I also had the honor of presenting our findings at the conference, an invaluable experience in communicating technical concepts and engaging with experts.

What I Learned

This project instilled a drive for excellence through relentless perseverance, proving that innovation emerges from pushing through complex academic and technical challenges. This blend of technical tenacity, collaborative spirit, and a commitment to delivering impactful results has prepared me to thrive in the creative, fast-paced environment of the Apple Developer Academy.
