Project Summary

DANAin is a prototype hybrid crowdfunding platform designed to bridge the funding gap for young entrepreneurs and SMEs in Indonesia. It directly connects innovators with investors, creating a more accessible, transparent, and inclusive investment ecosystem.

My Impact & Initiative

My greatest impact was taking the initiative to build a fully functional frontend prototype independently, even before the backend API was ready. I simulated the entire user experience — including complex workflows like dual-role registration, investment mechanisms, and a notification system using localStorage. This proactive step not only accelerated development but also ensured the UI was robust and intuitive from day one.

What I Learned

This project taught me the importance of technical independence and end-to-end project ownership. My dual role as a developer and UX researcher was invaluable in learning how to translate user needs into elegant technical solutions. I also learned that tight collaboration between design, development, and business planning is key to creating a product that solves real-world problems.
